Food Allergens and Intolerances

Course Overview

After taking Rich's course on Food Allergens & Intolerances, participants will be able to recognize reaction symptoms and create an effective plan to reduce reactions. This will allow participants to safely prepare, purchase and store food, and confidently serve students.
